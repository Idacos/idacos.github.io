Nail Size Guide

The most important aspect of of press on nails is finding your size!

Here are 3 ways to size your nails:

  1. Tape method:

    • Start by taking a piece of tape and placing it across your nail bed
    • Use a black marker or pen to place a dot on either side of your nail bed
    • Remove the tape and use a ruler with milimeter(mm) to measure your nails
    • Write down your measurement and repeat for all fingers

    *This method is recommended but may not guarantee accurate sizing

  2. Coin method:

    With your hand palm down on a flat surface, place a quarter to the right of your hand and take a photo. Ensure all nail beds are clearly visible.

    *This method does not guarantee accurate size, only a good estimate

  3. Purchase a sizing kit:

    Sizing kits are available to purchase to ensure the fit of your nails.

    *This method is guaranteed to give you the most accurate size.
