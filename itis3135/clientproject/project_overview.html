Project Overview

Description

This website is designed to promote a nail painting business in which users can purchase premade and custom painted nails.

Intended Users

  • Potential clients seeking nail art
  • Current clients interested in custom made nail art
  • Anyone with a fashion forward sense

Overview of the Website Content

  • Home: Welcome message and introduction to the business
  • Gallery: Photo gallery of nail designs including prices
  • Nail Size Guide: Provide information on how to measure nail sizes for accurate fitting
  • Custom Order Form: Form for custom orders
  • About Us: Information about the business and its owner
  • Contact Us: Contact information about the business and owner

Page Design Information

  • Home

    • Purpose: Welcome customers to the site and provide a home for the other pages
    • Audience/Users: Customers
    • Content: Introduction and brief description of products
    • Data Entry: No
    • Validations: No
    • Elements: Navigation bar
    • Actions: Button click navigates to other pages.

  • Gallery

    • Purpose: Display a photo gallery of nail designs, including prices.
    • Audience/Users: Customers
    • Content: Images of nail designs with prices and descriptions.
    • Data Entry: No
    • Validations: No
    • Elements: Image gallery and filters
    • Actions: Users click on images for details or use filters to view specific categories.

  • Nail Size Guide

    • Purpose: Provide information on how to measure nail sizes for accurate fitting.
    • Audience/Users: Customers
    • Content: Instructions on how to measure one's nails
    • Data Entry: No
    • Validations: No
    • Elements: Text
    • Actions: Users can make use of instructions to find their nail measurements

  • Custom Order Form

    • Purpose: Allow users to place custom orders for nail designs.
    • Audience/Users: Customers
    • Content: Form fields for user information, design preferences, measurements, and upload option for reference images.
    • Data Entry: Yes
    • Validations: Required fields for name, email, and order details.
    • Elements: Input fields, dropdowns for design options, file upload button, and a submit button.
    • Actions: Form submission processes the order

  • About Us

    • Purpose: Provide information about the business and its owner.
    • Audience/Users: Customers
    • Content: Business history, owner bio, and mission statement
    • Data Entry: No
    • Validations: No
    • Elements: Text, images, and social media links.
    • Actions: Users can click social media links to view more.

  • Contact Us

    • Purpose: Provides customers contact information for the business and owner.
    • Audience/Users: Customers
    • Content: Email and contact form
    • Data Entry: Yes
    • Validations: Required fields for name, email, and message.
    • Elements: Input fields, textarea for message, and a submit button.
    • Actions: Form submission sends the user's message

Dynamic Functionality

The website includes an interactive photo gallery on the "Gallery" page, utilizing jQuery to cycle through images when hovered. The "Custom Order Form" page features a form where users can enter their information to order custom painted nails. AJAX is used to load new designs without refreshing the page.
