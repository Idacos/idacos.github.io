About Us

Welcome to Painted by Pixi, where your nails become a canvas for creativity! Painted by Pixi was established in 2023 by Marisa Flowers. She has been an artist for over 10 years. Her passion for nail art shines through with every brush stroke. Marisa ensures that each set is a handmade masterpiece.

“I believe that nails are not just an accessory; they are a form of self expression” - Marisa Flowers
