Evaluation of Destinee Brewers's Website

Website Checklist - von Briesen's Web Classes

  • Is the website responsive and mobile-friendly?
  • Does it follow good design principles (CRAP - Contrast, Repetition, Alignment, Proximity)?
  • Are the fonts easy to read and are the colors visually appealing?
  • Is there a clear navigation menu and does it work correctly?
  • Does the website load quickly and without issues?
  • Is there relevant content with a clear focus on the website’s purpose?
  • Does the website have all necessary elements (e.g., header, footer, content, contact info)?
  • Is the website accessible (e.g., alt text for images, appropriate contrast ratios)?
  • Does the website have a clear call to action (e.g., contact form, shop)?
  • Are there any broken links or missing pages?

My Evaluation:

  • What’s working well: The website's theme is great and fits the company well.
  • What needs improvement: The formatting of the contact form could be adjusted to be a bit more even on both sides.
  • Suggestions for enhancement: Personally, I'd have it so all pages are available to click at the top rather than having them replaced with the page you're currently on.

Additional Comments:

Great job overall! The theme is beautiful and showcases the client well!
