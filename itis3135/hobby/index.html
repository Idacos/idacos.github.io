Best Times

Release Events

The best times to get some Pokémon cards is generally during set releases! New sets in the United States are generally released every two months, with occasional mini-sets added in between. Each set provides generally 200+ new cards that both collectors and players can make use of!

For those interested in competing, there are tournaments hosted year long by the Pokémon Company International in which anyone can register for and compete in! However, if competing for official tournaments may seem daunting, plenty of local card shops host their own tournaments for their community. This makes it easy for new players to get their foot into the door of competitive Pokémon.

Best Locations

Local Stores

The best places to find Pokémon cards are often local stores like Walmart, Target, and Gamestop. Though if you're able to, finding a local card shop might be the best option, as you can buy singles there, while also finding other people in the Pokémon TCG community to meet and hang out with.

One of the more popular card shops is known as Full Grip Games. They're located in Akron, Ohio, and gained its popularity due to the youtuber and twitch streamer TrickyGym.

How

Getting Started

If you're just looking to collect, getting into the Pokémon TCG is extremely easy. I suggest by starting off with just picking up some product at a local store near you and open the packs inside of it! One of the best products to pick up is what is know as an Elite Trainer Box (or ETB for short). It generally provides 10 packs to open, some sleeves, dice, and coins which can be used in anyway you'd like, along with a booklet that lists each card in the set, which can help you to start collecting!

If you'd want to get into the TCG competitively however, there are a few more steps you'd want to take. One of the best products to pick up for a new competitor is what is known as the league battle decks. They will often provide you with a strong deck to get you started along with dice, a rule book, and what to do in order to upgrade your new deck even futher!

Why

Nostalgia and Fun

For many people, collecting Pokémon cards is often about nostalgia. As Pokémon being such a large intellectual property, many young adults today are now collecting Pokémon cards out of nostalgia, as they would own Pokémon cards as a kid.

While nostalgia may impact me, the main reason as to why I play the Pokémon TCG is because I find it extremely fun! It's very simple to get into but still allows for depth and complexity the further you get into it, making is easy for both beginners and veterans to find enjoyment in the game.
